## Loading synths in Sonic Pi

Loading a new synth, created in Supercollider is very simple, just use load_synthdefs "path" and call the synth:

```ruby
load_synthdefs "C:/Users/.../SYNTHS/"
synth 'piTest'

play 60
sleep 1
play 50
sleep 2
```
